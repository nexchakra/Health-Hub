**HealthHub India** is a high-fidelity, industrial-grade pharmaceutical platform. Designed with a "Surveillance & Telemetry" aesthetic, it bridges the gap between traditional pharmacy services and cutting-edge AI-driven clinical oversight.

---

## üíé The Vision
Transforming the Indian pharmaceutical landscape by providing transparent batch surveillance, digital twin medicine guides, and real-time clinical telemetry for every patient.

## üåü Core Modules

### 1. Patient Surveillance Portal
*   **Smart Basket**: Intelligent checkout with logic for both **Safe Home Delivery** and **Express Store Takeaway**.
*   **Digital Twin Medicine Guide**: Interactive QR-based packs that provide usage videos and missed-dose recovery protocols.
*   **Health Insights**: Real-time tracking of medical fulfillment history and adherence metrics.
*   **Alternative Salt Discovery**: Automatically identifies clinically equivalent, cost-effective generic alternatives.

### 2. Clinical Operations (Staff) Dashboard
*   **Batch Surveillance**: Real-time telemetry on inventory levels and expiry monitoring.
*   **RX Verification Node**: Dedicated queue for pharmacists to review and authorize high-risk prescriptions.
*   **Supply Chain Registry**: Management of vendor reliability scores and automated Purchase Order (PO) workflows.
*   **Immutable Audit Logs**: Cryptographically hashed logs of every dispensing action for compliance.

### 3. AI Clinical Assistant (Gemini 3 Pro)
*   **Interaction Surveillance**: High-precision detection of drug-drug interactions.
*   **Dosage Telemetry**: Advanced reasoning for pediatric and geriatric dosage calculations.
*   **Voice-Activated Search**: Hands-free medicine discovery using natural language processing.
